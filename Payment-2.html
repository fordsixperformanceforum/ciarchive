Payment Information

ONLINE (Paypal or Credit Card)
For those who order online, you have three payment options.... PayPal, Authorize.net, or US Mail. This is the easiest and fastest way to place an order. It's also the most accurate. Simply place your order online, then choose the preferred payment option during the check out process.

BY MAIL (Money Order or Cashiers Check)
For those who prefer to place their order by mail, rather than online, you can make your payment using a money order or cashiers check. Simply place your order online using the shopping cart, calculate the shipping, print a copy of the order, then select the appropriate payment method (by mail). Then send a copy of your order, along with a money order or cashiers check, to Classic Inlines (see address below).

BY PHONE (Credit Card)
While we prefer that you place your order online, we will accept orders over the phone. The best way too place an order by phone, is to start by placing your order online using the shopping cart. By doing it this way, the shipping fee's are calculated automatically and a copy of the order is sent to us electronically, which helps to reduce errors. You can also print a copy of the order for your records. Then all you need to do is call and give us your credit card info over the phone. Once the payment has been processed you will receive confirmation by email, which shows the payment details.

Payment Methods

PayPal (echeck, balance transfer, or credit card)
Classic Inlines is setup to accept SECURE credit and debit card payments through PayPal, as well as balance transfers for existing PayPal users. PayPal guarantees the safety of your payment information, and your credit card information is NEVER stored online or forwarded to Classic Inlines for any reason.

Authorize.net (credit card)
Classic Inlines is setup to accept SECURE credit/debit card payments via Authorize.net, over the phone, and/or online. Unlike other companies, Classic Inlines does not keep a record of your credit/debit card information and it is NEVER stored online. As such, you will need to give us the information each and every time you place an order.

US Mail (money order or cashiers check)
If you submit an order via E-Mail or US Mail, you can send your payment (cashiers check or money order) to the address listed below. Please note that this method of ordering may be slower due to the time required to mail your payment. We do not accept personal checks, unless previous arrangements have been made and agreed to. In the event that we do decide to accept a personal check, your order will not be shipped until the check has cleared your bank account, which normally takes 7-10 business days. No exceptions.
