Welcome
First time visitor? If so, please take a few minutes to read our welcome page.

Thank you for visiting Classic Inlines. We hope you find our website interesting, informative, and educational. As well as helpful, assisting you in your selection of performance products for your inline six. From simple quick bolt on modifications, to all out performance rebuilds, we offer only the highest quality performance products available, at the lowest possible prices. Many of our products are produced by the industry's leading manufacturers, and in some cases, these products are offered exclusively through Classic Inlines. We also design, and manufacture our own products. By utilizing the best engineers and pattern makers, foundries and machinists, and the latest technology, our products meet or exceed our high standards and those of our customers.

One of the first things you'll notice about the new site, is the addition of many new products, or categories. If the product is not currently available, you are linked to the Coming Soon page. We apologize for this inconvenience, but did so in an effort to inform our customers of all the new and exciting products we will be adding to our site over the coming years. Our goal is to eventually stock every possible part you might need, to complete your inline six project, rather than just offering performance engine products. Our customers can rest assured they are purchasing the best quality their money can buy, at the very best prices. As Classic Inlines policy is to seek out and offer only the highest quality products available.

Classic Inlines refuses to offer inferior products or cheap imitations. If we find we are offering a product which does not meet our high standards, the product is removed from our product line and no longer offered. Occasionally, under rare circumstances, we may continue to offer a specific product simply because there are no other options available. In some cases, Classic Inlines may pursue manufacturing the product in house, in hopes of producing an item that will meets our high standards.
Classic Inlines is constantly designing and developing new products, and chatting with various manufacturers about producing their products for our inline sixes. Last year we introduced several new items including our alloy valve covers, the DUI distributor & Livewires, and our dual out shortly style header. The coming year looks very promising with the introduction of our new aluminum cylinder head right around the corner. We will also be introducing various intake manifolds, as well as turbo and supercharging kits, fuel injection kits and components, and many more exciting products.
