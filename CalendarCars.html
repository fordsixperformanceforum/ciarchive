Calendar Cars

Classic Inlines is happy to display what we call "Calendar Cars", meaning they would be suitable for a Classic Inlines Calendar. Once we get a dozen, we'll put together a calendar for the following year. Hopefully we can make this an annual event.

The cars will be featured in the gallery, and the owners will receive a $50.00 gift certificate for use on a future purchases, once the calendars are produced. This is just our way of paying tribute to our customers and their inline rides. Send in your pics and stats, via e-mail, so we can get them posted. Thanks
