Header Applications

Verify the headers will fit before you order!

If your vehicle is not listed below and you would like to order one of our headers, we will be happy to work with you. In the event the header shipped to you does not fit, FSPP will accept it for return, either for another style or a full refund. We will also pay for the return shipping. In return, if they do fit, we ask you to let us know so we can add your vehicle type to the charts for future customer reference. Thank You.

Not sure how to tell which starter you have?

Low starters are below the oil pan gasket rim.
High starters are above the oil pan rim (parallel to the freeze plugs).
