International Shipping Policies

Please read our Domestic Shipping policies, as well as the additional International Shipping policies stated below.

While our automatic shipping calculator does a pretty good job calculating shipping charges within the Continental USA, it has a difficult time with International orders. You may place an order using the automatic calculator, in which case, Classic Inlines will do everything possible to ship your order for the calculated cost. However, Classic Inlines will occasionally need to manually calculate the shipping charges. This also applies to items where the shipping charges were included in the published price, as in most cases, it only applies to orders shipped within the Continental USA unless otherwise stated or offered as a shipping option.

Once the actual shipping cost have been determined, Classic Inlines will notify the customer of any additional charges via e-mail, if needed, and will send a request for the additional funds via PayPal or other acceptable means of payment. If the customer does not agree to accept responsibility for the additional shipping charges, the order will be canceled and all monies will be refunded.

We apologize for the inconvenience, however we believe it is better than eliminating International Orders altogether. Classic Inlines will do everything possible to assist you in placing an international order and getting it shipped for the lowest possible price. Usually, our USPS postal shipping is the cheapest way to ship your order, but you are welcome to look elsewhere to see if other arrangements might be better.

Classic Inlines is also in the process of setting up International Dealers in Australia, Argentina, Brazil, and Finland. By doing so, these dealers may be able to ship your order from their location, reducing the shipping expenses. However, the selection of products stocked will be limited to those items which are in the greatest demand, such as our aluminum cylinder heads, intakes, headers, camshafts, rocker assemblies, valve covers, timing sets, etc.

If you are interested in becoming an International Dealer, please contact us via e-mail or give us a call.
